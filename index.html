Welcome to the 2014 JDAI Reports Submission System


Welcome to the 2014 JDAI Annual Reports Portal. The portal is currently under construction however, on October 10, 2014 all sites will be able to enter results data. To access and print a copy of your site’s data, please use the access code provided to your site last year.

When the portal is ready to received reports, all sites will be notified. If you have questions regarding the portal or desire to share feedback related to this site, please do not hesitate to contact me at gmumford@aecf.org or Tom Woods at twoods@aecf.org.

We look forward to receiving another wonderful data set.

All the best,

Gail D. Mumford
